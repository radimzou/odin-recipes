Szeged goulash from pork

Description

Do you need some food for good mood during freezing evenings?

This special Szeged goulash is here just for you!

Ingredients (4 portions)

  • 1 kg of pork neck meat without bones
  • 4 big onions
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 0.5 kg of sauerkraut
  • red pepper spice
  • spicy red pepper or chilli spice
  • black pepper spice powder
  • salt
  • cumin
  • broth
  • marjoram
  • flour
  • pork fat

Steps

  1. At first we need to prepare our meat - cut it to semi-small pieces (about 5 cm)
  2. Chop the onions to soft pieces
  3. In a big pot fry roughly meat pieces in order to close the meat juice in it
  4. Once the meat is roughly fried, put the meat out of the pot onto a plate
  5. Then add a fat to the pot and fry onion pieces very slow for more than 20 minutes. Stearing is must have! During this frying add some cumin and salt
  6. Then add half of minced garlic cloves and fry it for 2 minutes
  7. Then add whole amount of red pepper and stear it for 3 minutes
  8. Once this mixture is fried well add the meat into the pot with black pepper (also with the leftover juice from the plate)
  9. Stear it for more than 5 minutes
  10. Add a broth (reduced with hot water or fully prepared before)
  11. Boil it for more than 90 minuts on a soft fire
  12. Add some spice according to you taste (more salt, pepper, cumin)
  13. Once a taste is comfort, turn off the fire on a cooker and let it be for the whole night
  14. Next day start cooking again: turn on the cooker
  15. Once goulash is boiling grab the fat layer from the surface of the goulash into a mug = this is called "Glanc"
  16. Mix some flour with water in another mug and add it into a pot in order to goulash be more thick. Boile for more than 10 minutes
  17. Put the "Glanc" back into the pot, add the rest of the minced garlic and marjoram.
  18. Put the "Glanc" back into the pot, add the rest of the minced garlic and marjoram.
  19. Boil for another 2 minutes and YOUR GOULASH IS READY :-)
  20. For side dish prepare some dumplings
  21. Bon apetite! :-)
